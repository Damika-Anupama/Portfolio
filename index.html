FlashGuard ‚ö°

FlashGuard is one of best projects I've ever worked on with 3 of my friends. Here we use an IOT device with a mobile app to provide real-time protection against seizure triggers in individuals with photosensitive epilepsy. It integrates a camera, microcontroller, and adaptive electrochromic lenses, with a mobile app and cloud technology for personalized settings. I'm pleased to contribute this project.
