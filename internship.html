Internship - WealthOS Lanka Pvt. (Ltd)

WealthOS is a UK-based company that aims to provide a cloud-native, enterprise wealth management platform for people worldwide¹[1]. The company was founded in October 2019 and has raised $800,000 in funding. The company has a diverse team of founders, engineers, product managers, and subject matter experts from various countries and backgrounds. The company follows agile methodologies and uses tools and services to facilitate communication and collaboration.

The technical stack used by WealthOS includes:

  1. AWS Lambdas: The core functionality of the platform is built on serverless technology using AWS Lambdas, which are functions that run in response to events and automatically scale up or down.
  2. React: The Operations Center, which is the user interface for the platform, is built on React, a JavaScript library for creating user interfaces.
  3. REST APIs: The platform exposes well-designed REST APIs for integrating with third-party providers and services, such as Onfido, Stripe, Morningstar, etc²[2]. The APIs are also documented using tools like Swagger.
  4. Other tools and services: The platform uses a host of other tools and services as part of the build, launch, and operate process, such as GitHub, Jira, Slack, Jenkins, Terraform, etc.

Life at WealthOS

I joined the company in December 2023 as a full stack software engineer intern. I'm working with a team of experienced engineers, product managers, and subject matter experts. The company follows agile methodologies and uses tools and services to facilitate communication and collaboration. I'm working on a cloud-native platform built on cutting-edge serverless technology using AWS Lambdas. The Operations Center is built on React, and the platform exposes well-designed REST APIs for integrating with third-party providers and services.

The staff members at WealthOS are very friendly and helpful. They come from diverse backgrounds, and they are always willing to lend a helping hand. The working place is new and modern, with state-of-the-art facilities that are designed to help me work efficiently and comfortably.
