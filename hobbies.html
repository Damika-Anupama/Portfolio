Play Music Instruments

I am a three-instrumentalist. They are the violin and tabla, two eastern musical instruments, and the acoustic guitar. In sixth grade, I began to play the violin and tabla. I now hold a Praveshika certificate in both tabla and violin. In addition, I'm like the rhythm that comes from my music. In the orchestras at my school and university, I played violin and guitar, respectively.

I started playing the guitar mostly to socialize with people, especially on picnics and trips.Playing these instruments truly helps me de-stress from programming and academics.
